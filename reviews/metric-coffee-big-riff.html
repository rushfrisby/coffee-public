Metric Coffee Big Riff

Reviewed on Thursday, April 29, 2021
Score
67

I can't say that this roast delivered as big of a riff as I was expecting with it's subdued acidity and light body. I did get a little buttery-ness from it which was nice.

The smell of tobacco, chocolate, and almond is a good combination however the cholocate disappears when you taste it. The aftertaste is a bit smoky.

When I made a latte with it I got a teeny amount of a tea aroma.

It can be used as an espresso but it leans more towards a medium roast. Overall it was pretty good an can see it being a worthy contender as a daily drink for many people.

Tags: tobacco chocolate almond smoky light tea
