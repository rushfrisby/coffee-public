Foxtail Coffee Midnight Oil

Reviewed on Thursday, May 6, 2021
Score
61

Midnight Oil smells like raspberry and dark raisin. It has a rounded acidity and syrupy body. It tastes like dried fruit, mostly raisin, and has a similar aftertaste. The roast is dark.

Overall I think it fell short of my expectations. Foxtail is touted as one of the best roasters in Orlando but you wouldn't know it by tasting this coffee. I find it hard to justify the cost based on the result. The shipping cost higher than most roasters I order from too. If you get on Foxtail's mailing list they send out discount codes regularly so I suggest waiting for one of those to try it.
