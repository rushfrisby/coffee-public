Lucky Goat Coffee Euro Espresso

Reviewed on Tuesday, May 4, 2021
Score
63

So the espresso in Europe is different that the US? Well I have to check this out and see for myself...

When I got the coffee I noticed that the beans were roasted extremely dark and were very oily.

It smells like black liquorice and almonds but comes off lively and sharp. The body is heavy and buttery. The flavor ended up being more of a pomegranate and rubber taste which I found inconsistent with the aroma. The aftertaste is spicy with and slightly smoky.

This roast is very dark!

The boldness of producing such a dark roast is awesome but not for me. Let the Euro's keep this one.

If you order $25 or more you get free shipping so if you end up buying this grab another flavor to try along with it.
