Lucky Goat Coffee Candy Bar

Reviewed on Monday, May 3, 2021
Score
60

The aroma of this coffee is very strong! When they say "Candy Bar" they mean it... it smells like a Heath Bar, toffee, and caramel. It's sharp and has a heavy and syrupy body. It tastes like pipe tobacco and caramel with the pipe tobacco aftertaste carrying through with some bitterness at the end.

The smell is so strong that it is a little gimmicky. If you make a latte with this the milk with smooth out the pipe tobacco taste and bitterness. Add a little less sugar than normal if you make a latte. Overall I found the flavor enjoyable but wouldn't drink it regularly.

If you order $25 or more you get free shipping so if you end up buying this grab another flavor to try along with it.

Tags: heath-bar toffee caramel sharp pipe-tobacco heavy syrupy medium-roast
