Tugboat Coffee 1699 Blend

Reviewed on Wednesday, May 5, 2021
Score
74

This blend gives of a sweet aroma of rose pedals and malt. It is bright and lively with a light body. The flavor is combination of floral and cereal with a roasted aftertaste.

The consistency of aroma from beans and ground coffee to the taste doesn't change much. It tastes just like it smells. Overall this is a solid choice. I'm looking forward to trying more roasts from Tugboat!

Tags: rose malt bright lively floral cereal
