Bow Truss Coffee Foundation Espresso

Reviewed on Monday, May 10, 2021
Score
76

It's like standard espresso with a twist of lime. It's not overpowering though. The taste is amazing! You can even smell the lime a little bit. It blends well with the caramel aroma and flavor. It comes off lively and crisp. The body is syrupy which makes it a real treat. The aftertaste is a little grainy.

When I made a latte with it the lime zest still came through.

Overall the lime zest makes this coffee truely unique and borderline exotic. I highly recommending giving it a try. I will definately be keeping a stock of this on-hand at all times!

Tags: lime caramel lively crisp light syrupy
