Starbucks Odyssey Blend

Reviewed on Friday, May 7, 2021
Score
50

I didn't find anything special about this coffee. There's nothing bad about it and it's better than your standard Folgers classic roast if that's what you drink regularly and want to step-up your daily game.

I found it to have a sunflower seed and prune smell. There is a sharp acidity and thick body. The flavor is woody with a slightly acidic & sour taste. Afterwards the acidity does linger.

I picked this up at my local Starbucks. I don't think they have it at every store - you can check online using their website.

Tags: sunflower-seeds prune sharp thick woody sour acidic
