Bones Coffee Company Electric Unicorn

Reviewed on Saturday, May 22, 2021
Score
52

If you like gimmicky coffee this is your winner. The smell is so potent that it permeates through the packaging. First and foremost it smells like Fruit Loops which a hint of chocolate covered cherries.

It's lively and crisp with a syrupy mouthfeel.

The flavor is... All. Fruit. Loops.

It leaves a dry fruity taste in the mouth as if you just had a bowl of cereal. The roast is medium.

Personally I can't stand the smell and threw the remaining beans away immediately after tasting. The smell and flavor are intense and I only recommend this if you enjoy that sort of thing.

Trader Joe's Espresso Blend

Reviewed on Monday, May 17, 2021
Score
40

"Organic Fair Trade 5 Country Espresso Blend" is a mouthful so I'm just going to call it Trader Joe's Espresso Blend. Do they have other espresso blends? I'm not sure and I don't think I want to waste my time finding out after how bad this coffee was.

The aroma is actually quite nice. It smells like sweet raisin and brown spice. I had a hopeful ambition that this would be a good tasting coffee to start off with.

The acidity is flat and the body is thick.

The flavor and after taste is where this coffee completely bombs. It tastes like nutmeg and rubber and leaves the rubber after taste in your mouth. Disgusting!

After the cupping I threw away the beans I had left over. Yep, it's that bad.

I picked this batch up in my local store. Trader Joe's doesn't have it on their website.

Avoid at all costs.

Tags: raisin fair-trade blend espresso brown-spice nutmeg rubber

Roaster: Trader Joe's Amount: 13oz Price: $8.99

Opus Coffee Organic East Timor

Reviewed on Thursday, May 13, 2021
Score
62

The bean are very dark and oily and smell like herbs and molasses which was a great indicator of what the coffee tastes like.

Once the beans brew the coffee smells less like molasses and more like tea. It's sharp on the tongue yet has a watery body. The flavor is very dark and tastes like black tea. There is a fleeting aftertaste of tea flavor at the end.

For a dark roast I like that it is watery and resembled tea because it lightens it up a bit.

Tags: organic herbs molasses sharp watery dark-roast black-tea

Opus Coffee Bali Blue Moon

Reviewed on Wednesday, May 12, 2021
Score
65

This coffee smells like grapefruit and grain. It is tart, dry, and has a light body. The flavor tastes just as it smells with the grapefruit and grain coming through to the end with a fruity citrus aftertaste.

It's a light roast and the citrus flavor isn't as strong it sounds. The grain balances everything out. Overall I enjoyed this coffee.

Opus Coffee Espresso Blend

Reviewed on Tuesday, May 11, 2021
Score
82

My favorite coffee. It's the one I compare all others to.

Everyone I grind the beans around says how much they love the smell. The aroma of dark chocolate, cherry, and brown sugar is absolutely amazing! Combine that with a drink that is lively when it hits your tongue and has a light body - this coffee will blow your mind! The flavor delivers a huge taste of chocolate and cherry and has a roasted & sweet aftertaste.

This is how all coffee should taste!
