Blond Giraffe Cafe Cubano

Reviewed on Monday, June 21, 2021
Score
63

The aroma is sweet and nice. It smells floral, like jasmine and brown sugar. It's subdued and syrupy. It's got a little bit of butteriness to it at the end. When brewed, a burnt dark sugar flavor dominates the jasmine and aftertaste is similar.

It's a dark roast. The beans are dark and shiny but not excessively oily. The aroma and body are the best part of this coffee. It would be fantastic if it wasn't roasted as long and was a dark-to-medium roast.

Tags: floral jasmine brown-sugar subdued syrupy buttery burnt dark-roast

Bones Coffee Company Shark Bite

Reviewed on Wednesday, June 9, 2021
Score
53

Shark Bite smells like caramel and chocolate covered cherries. It's sharp on the tongue and has a light body. The flavor is woody and has sour dark chocolate notes. The wooden and sourness taste carries through to the aftertaste.

It doesn't taste as gimmicky as it smells but I'd still call it gimmicky because the smell is too harsh. The sourness stands out and is a turn-off.

Tags: caramel chocolate-cherries gimmicky sharp light dark-chocolate woody sour

Bones Coffee Company Chocolate Raspberry

Reviewed on Wednesday, June 9, 2021
Score
39

The chocolate and raspberry smell is strong and disgusting. You can smell it through the packaging. It's another one of Bones Coffee exceedingly gimmicky flavors. I threw the remaining beans I had in the garbage after the cupping which I debated even doing.

That being said, the acidity is subdued and the body is light and a little buttery.

The flavor and aftertaste is all raspberry.

Bones Coffee Company High Voltage

Reviewed on Wednesday, May 26, 2021
Score
58

High Voltage smells like apples, caramel, and has a hint of cherry. It's crisp on the tongue and has a full body. The flavor carries through from the aroma and tastes like apple and caramel. The fruit flavor stays with you for a moment afterwards.

Unlike other Bones Coffee flavors, High Volatage is not gimmicky at all. I found it completely enjoyable and would order it again. The apple flavor is most prominent but subtle.

Bones Coffee Company S'morey Time

Reviewed on Monday, May 24, 2021
Score
56

This coffee smells like cocoa and walnut. The acidity is flat and somewhat tart. It has a thick body and the flavor is just as you'd imagine. The cocoa and nutty aftertaste carries though to the end.

If you add a little bit of sugar and top it off with a few marshmallows you'd never know you were drinking coffee. It's a little bit gimmicky for me but not too bad. I might get this again around the holidays for an after-meal treat.

Tags: cocoa walnut flat tart thick nutty medium-roast
